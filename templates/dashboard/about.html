Our Story

Founded in the beautiful valleys of Nepal, Everest Beauty was born from a passion for authentic beauty and a desire to share the treasures of the Himalayas with the world.

Our journey began when we discovered the incredible potential of natural ingredients sourced from the pristine mountains and valleys of Nepal. From traditional skincare secrets passed down through generations to modern formulations that meet international standards, we've created a platform that celebrates both heritage and innovation.

Today, we're proud to connect customers with the finest beauty products from Nepal, supporting local communities and preserving traditional beauty wisdom for future generations.
